B.S.Ind.Ed.Interior Environmental Design

Interior design is the design of the interior space of a building, taking into account its usability, aesthetics, and safety, with an emphasis on creating an environment suitable for human use. This curriculum is designed for individuals interested in pursuing both a teaching profession and an interior design profession. Students gain practical experience through professional internships and cooperative education programs, enabling them to truly understand and perform effectively in various roles, including teacher or lecturer in both public and private educational institutions, and as an interior environmental designer.
